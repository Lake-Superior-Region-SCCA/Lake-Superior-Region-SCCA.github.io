How the SOLO series points are calculated

The SOLO series points are calculated as follows:
(low PAX time of the day/your low PAX time) x 100 = your points for the event
For every three events a competitor participates in they may drop their lowest event.

The points system used for 2008 and 2009 was similar to the old NASCAR points system, with 175 points to the winner, minus 5 points per position for positions 2-5, 4 less points per position for positions 6-10, and 3 less points per position for positions 11 on down.
